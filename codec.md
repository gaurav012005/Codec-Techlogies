# Project Allocation Schedule (28 Days)

Based on the complexity ratings of our current projects (Two projects at **11/10** complexity, and Three projects at **8/10** complexity), here is the optimized 28-day time allocation plan.

## Overview
We have a total of **28 days** to complete the following **5 unique projects**:

1. **AI-Powered Recruitment ATS Platform** (High Complexity)
2. **Online Examination Platform** (High Complexity)
3. **Enterprise CRM System** (Medium Complexity)
4. **Smart Inventory & Supply Chain Management System** (Medium Complexity)
5. **Real-Time Logistics & Fleet Tracking Platform** (Medium Complexity)

*(Note: There are two directories for the Online Examination Platform, which are duplicates. We are scheduling for 5 unique projects).*

## Methodology
We calculate the distribution based on the complexity points out of 10:
- **Total Points**: (2 projects × 11 points) + (3 projects × 8 points) = 22 + 24 = **46 points**
- **Days per point**: 28 days / 46 points ≈ **0.608 days/point**
- **11/10 Project Allocation**: 11 × 0.608 ≈ **6.69 days** -> Supported with exactly **6.5 days**
- **8/10 Project Allocation**: 8 × 0.608 ≈ **4.87 days** -> Supported with exactly **5.0 days**
